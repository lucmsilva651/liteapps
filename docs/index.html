Lite Apps Repo

Welcome! Here you will find a variety of lightweight applications for Android, focusing on performance-limited devices. For example: If you have a J1 Mini or even a Moto C lying around, these apps are perfect for these devices. All are very well optimized, lightweight to download and install, and above all, SAFE. Go to the All apps section to choose and download one of them (or all of them as well). Or you can enter in our Telegram Channel for latest updates about new Lite Apps.
